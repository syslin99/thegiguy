Dr. Terry Lin has vast experience and training in the field of gastroenterology. After completing his fellowship training in general gastroenterology, he further advanced his training in transplant hepatology and advanced therapeutic endoscopy including therapeutic ERCP and various techniques for endoscopic tumor ablation therapy and stent placements.

He became a faculty member with the University of Colorado Health and Science Center. He eventually relocated to the Bay Area where he worked for San Jose Medical Group and was appointed Medical Director of San Jose Medical Group Endoscopy Center.

In 2003, he opened his private practice in the San Jose — Los Gatos Area. With his extensive training and experience, Dr. Lin is recognized for his advanced therapeutic endoscopy skills and knowledge in the field of gastroenterology and hepatology.

In addition to his clinical practice, Dr. Terry Lin has also been involved in researching new developments and has also received grants from various research projects. He has been invited as a guest speaker for many medical conferences.

Dr. Terry Lin is compassionate towards his patients and has pride in providing a higher quality of patient care. Throughout the years, he has received many commendations and referrals from his patients and colleagues.
